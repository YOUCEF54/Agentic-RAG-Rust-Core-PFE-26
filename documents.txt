The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France. It was built in 1889.
It stands 330 meters tall. It was designed by Gustave Eiffel.

The Great Wall of China stretches over 21,000 kilometers.
It was built over many centuries, starting in the 7th century BC.

The Amazon River is the largest river in the world by water flow.
It is located in South America and flows through Brazil.
